In Figma, projects are collections of related files organized to help teams collaborate efficiently on design work. A project acts as a container for various design files, prototypes, and assets. Projects can be organized by product, feature, team, or any other structure that best fits the workflow, and they can be shared with team members for real-time collaboration.
